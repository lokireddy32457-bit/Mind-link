Our Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based psychiatric care tailored to your unique needs. Every treatment plan is personalized to help you achieve lasting wellness.

Individual Psychotherapy

One-on-one therapy sessions using proven modalities to help you understand patterns, process emotions, and build resilience.

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
  • Psychodynamic Therapy
  • Mindfulness-Based Techniques
  • 45–60 minute sessions
💊

Medication Management

Expert evaluation, prescribing, and monitoring of psychiatric medications to find the right balance for your wellbeing.

  • Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluation
  • Antidepressant & Anti-Anxiety Medications
  • Mood Stabilizers & ADHD Medications
  • Ongoing Monitoring & Adjustments
  • Side-Effect Management
🖥️

Telehealth / Virtual Sessions

Receive the same quality of care from the comfort and convenience of your own home via secure video sessions.

  • HIPAA-Compliant Video Platform
  • Available Across All States Licensed
  • Flexible Scheduling
  • Same-Day Appointments Available
  • Ideal for Follow-Up Visits

Anxiety & Depression Treatment

Specialized care for anxiety disorders and depression using a combination of therapy and medication when appropriate.

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder
  • Social Anxiety & Phobias
  • Major Depressive Disorder
  • Panic Disorder
  • Treatment-Resistant Depression

PTSD & Trauma Therapy

Specialized trauma-informed care to help you process, heal, and move forward after traumatic experiences.

  • Trauma-Focused CBT
  • EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization)
  • Complex PTSD Treatment
  • Grief & Loss Counseling
  • Veterans & First Responders

Age-appropriate psychiatric care for young people navigating behavioral, emotional, and developmental challenges.

  • School-Related Challenges
  • Family Psychoeducation
